Privacy Policy

A timer that plays your music. Nothing more.
This app is a custom timer that can play your own music files during different phases of a session. It does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information about you. This page explains what that means in plain language.

Effective  April 25, 2026
Version  1.0
Applies to  the App
The short version
No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. No ads. The app runs a timer, reads music files you already own to play during the phases you choose, and uses your device's media controls so you can play, pause, and skip tracks. That's it.

What the App Does
The app is a customizable timer. You can configure phases (for example, work and rest intervals, rounds, warmups, cooldowns, or any custom segments) and assign your own music files to play during each phase. The timer and music playback run entirely on your device.

Information We Collect
None. The app does not collect any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, location, contacts, or analytics of any kind. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no servers we send information to.

Your timer configurations and music selections are saved locally on your device so they persist between sessions. They are never transmitted off the device.

Permissions the App Uses
The app requests two permissions strictly so it can function as a timer with music playback. Both are used locally on your device and neither results in any data leaving the device.

Music Files
To read audio files you already have on your device so the app can play them during your selected timer phases. The files are not uploaded, copied, modified, or shared.
Media Controls
To integrate with your device's standard playback controls (lock screen, headphones, Bluetooth, notification shade) so you can play, pause, and skip tracks while the timer runs.
How Your Music Files Are Handled
Files are read directly from your device's storage when a timer phase begins.
The app stores only a reference to the files you assign to phases — not a copy.
Files are never uploaded to any server, cloud, or third party.
The app does not modify, rename, move, or delete your files.
If you remove the app, your timer settings are removed with it; your music files remain untouched in their original location.
Third Parties
The app does not include third‑party advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with anyone — because there is no data being collected in the first place.

Notifications & Background Activity
The app may run in the background or display notifications so the timer continues to count down accurately and music playback isn't interrupted when your screen locks or you switch apps. These notifications are generated locally and contain no personal information.

Children's Privacy
Because the app does not collect any information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). The app is safe to use at any age.

Your Rights
Privacy laws such as the GDPR and CCPA grant you rights over your personal data — to access it, correct it, delete it, or stop its sale. Since we never collect or hold any personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, export, or delete on your behalf.

Changes to This Policy
If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds a feature that requires handling data — we will update this page and revise the "Effective" date above. Material changes will be reflected in the app's release notes.
